**COURSES AND THEIR OUTLINE **

  1. STA203 (Statistics for physical science) **
    - Set theory
    - Counting Method (permutations, combination, step diagram)
    - probability
    - probability distribution
    - conditional probability and Bayes theorem
  2. **MAT203 (Linear Algebra) **
    - vector space
    - basis and dimension
    - linear transformation
    - algebra of matrices
    - rings, null spaces and rank
  3. **MAT201 (Mathematical Method I) **
    - real value of real variable
    - review of differentiation, integration and their applications
    - mean value theorem and Taylor's series
  4. *CSC201 (Application of Computer Technology)**
    - Fundamental of computer Operations
    - Fundamental of word processing
    - Fundamental of Spreadsheet application
    - Fundamental of internet operations
  5. *CSC203 (Principle of programming I) **
    - C Language fundamentals
    - Array, Functions and Strings (For C language)
    - Pointers, structures, unions, file handling
  6. **CSC 205 (Assembly language programming )**
    - Introduction to Machine Level Language, distinction between high level language and assembly language
    - Organisations and use of memory , central processor, input/output devices, review of data and instructions representation mode
    - computer instructions set; modes of addressing
  7. *CSC 207 (Internet Technology and Programming I) **
    - General introduction to Internet and World Wide Web
    - Basics of HTML
    - HTML links
    - HTML images
    -HTML lists
    - HTML forms
    - HTML tables
  8. *GNS 203 (Innovation and product development) **
    - overview of creativity and innovation
    - idea process and opportunity recognition
    - creativity and innovation in organization
    - distinction between creativity and innovation
    - misinterpretation of innovation
    - sources of innovative business business opportunities
    - creativity and product development process
    - the market, the target, the customer
    - creativity management and creative manager
    - application of creativity and innovation to different fields/new product
  9. **GNS 101 (use of English)**
    - basic grammar : morpheme to sentence
    - parts of speech
    - concord
  10. *GNS103 (Computer Appreciation) **
    - Basic computing concept and xteristics
    - history of computer
    - classification of computer
    - advantage and disadvantages of computer
    - computer system
    Memory and its representation
    - software and its types
    - operating system
    - data presentation, its steps, its methods and its system
    - application of computer system in the society
    - computer network
    - network components - network topology
    - internet and its terminologies
